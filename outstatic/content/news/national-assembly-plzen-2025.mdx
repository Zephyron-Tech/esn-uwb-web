ESN UWB Pilsen is proud to have hosted their first National Assembly in November 2025. This platform has enabled ESN student volunteers to visit the city perhaps for their first time.

National Assemblies are the statutory events of ESN Czech Republic. We, as well as other ESN sections in the country, have our obligation to attend these events, as all of our voices and votes matter to aim the national level of the network in the right direction. It is also an opportunity for the leadership of ESN sections to meet to discuss serious topics in their communities.

We have discussed the Strategic Plan of ESN CZ 2026-29 to be approved later on, approved the Financial Results of mandate 2024-25, approved minor changes in statutes of ESN CZ and finally sent ESN UP Olomouc on their merry way to organise the next, electoral National Assembly in April, to which we will also be sending our members to represent the voice of our section.

Every National Event is a cause for celebrations, so we have also packed our evenings with social programme for all participants. On Friday evening we let ourselves be chased through the city, dropping clues for groups to find their wanted person, all the while exploring the city of Pilsen. On Saturday we moved our dinner time into Bar U Souseda for community building to play some games sing a song or two together, so that we could finally let off some steam after a very intense programme on that day.

National Assemblies are also a platform for our partners. Through the weekend we have received representatives from Megabooks, Sonnetor, Timetravels and EUPeace. We thank the University of West Bohemia to be most collaborative in making this event happen. Special thanks belongs to Lenka Pekárková in helping us secure the rooms and to dr. Magdaléna Rousová, Head of International Office of UWB, for formally welcoming the network on the grounds of our University.

We are thankful to the network for enabling us to organise such event and Vicepresident of ESN CZ, Jakub Starý for walking us through it step by step. Finally, it must be said that it couldn't have happened without the joyful and amazing oranisational committee made of the members of ESN UWB Pilsen and its alumni.

This event did not scare us enough to never want to touch it again and so when called into action, we signed up to organise Community Meetings Spring 2026 in Castle Nečtiny! Coming soon...
